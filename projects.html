Temples Timeline

The Temples Timeline app was featured in the October 2021 edition of BYU Studies Quarterly. The app is available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Temples Timeline displays a chronology of temples built by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from 1836 to the present day in a spiral layout, suitable for handheld touchscreen devices. I worked on this with Tsz Chin Lam and Litian Zhang, and our professor Dr. Geoff Draper.

Henriette

"Henriette" was my final project for CS 490R at BYU-Hawaii. "Henriette" tells the story of a mother hen who has lost her chicks. I developed the game over a one month span using Unity. It can be played here, and source code is available on GitHub along with a downloadable build.
